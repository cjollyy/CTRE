ABOUT

A background built on the ground, not behind a desk.

Charles was born and raised in Manchester, England. At age twelve, the family moved to Glasgow, Scotland, where he finished school and went on to complete an apprenticeship as a welder. Once that apprenticeship was done, he took a leap into an entirely different trade, and moved to Dubai with little in savings to begin selling real estate.

That start, with no local network and no safety net, set the pace for how he still works today. Every deal has been built the hard way, through direct contact, hard hours, and staying close to the detail, rather than relying on a big brokerage machine to generate the next lead.

Charles Thomas Real Estate carries that approach forward. One point of contact, from the first enquiry through to the keys changing hands. The name comes from Charles, and Thomas, the two names he goes by day to day, kept separate from the family name so nobody mistakes the business for a joke about being cheerful.

The current focus is Mira and Mira Oasis, two family communities in Reem where Charles has walked the streets enough times to know which row faces the afternoon sun. Coverage is growing across wider Dubai as new instructions come in.
